## ZEC Holders

Earn yield on ZEC without giving up custody. Timelock ZEC on the Zcash chain, delegate to a validator, and earn Z token rewards. Or bridge ZEC to Z for liquid staking via stZEC, which can be used as collateral on [[Z Lend]].

**Start with:** [[Staking Overview]] · [[ZEC Staking]] · [[Liquid Staking]]

---

## DeFi Users

Trade, lend, borrow, and earn yield with privacy. Z's [[The Zcosystem|Zcosystem]] provides a full application stack integrated with the [[ShieldedPool (MASP)]]. Use [[USDZ]] as a private stablecoin, deposit into [[yUSDZ]] for yield, or trade on [[Z Trade]].

**Start with:** [[The Zcosystem]] · [[USDZ]] · [[Z Trade]] · [[Z Lend]]

---

## Developers

Deploy Solidity contracts on Z without modification. Standard EVM tooling (Hardhat, Foundry, ethers.js) works out of the box. Two additional precompiles (Equihash and Blake2b) support Zcash verification but do not affect standard application development.

---

## AI Agents

Z provides scaffolding for agents to build and operate on-chain economies. Protocol-level wallet hygiene via the [[ShieldedPool (MASP)|ShieldedPool]], stealth addresses, a vertically integrated DeFi stack, and agent-native tooling (MCP servers, SKILL.md files).

---

## Zcash Miners

Earn Z token rewards by embedding delegation metadata in the blocks you already mine. No new hardware, no additional energy. Include a validator address and reward address in an OP_RETURN output.

**Start with:** [[Staking Overview]] · [[Zcash Satoshi Plus (ZSP)]]

---

## Validators

Get elected through hybrid scoring that combines miner delegation, ZEC holder delegation, and Z token delegation. Register through the CandidateHub contract by staking a minimum Z token deposit and providing a consensus key.

**Start with:** [[Zcash Satoshi Plus (ZSP)]]

---

## Compliance Teams

Privacy coexists with regulatory requirements. Viewing keys provide selective, time-scoped, read-only disclosure. Association Set Providers screen deposits and withdrawals against sanctions lists. Privacy Pools let users prove fund cleanliness without revealing identity.

**Start with:** [[Privacy and Compliance]]
